## This is a Premium Resource

The CS Maturity Self-Assessment is a comprehensive 30-question diagnostic tool that evaluates your organization across six dimensions:

1. **Strategy & Vision** - Executive alignment and CS mandate clarity
2. **People & Organization** - Team structure, skills, and career paths
3. **Process & Operations** - Workflow maturity and scalability
4. **Technology & Data** - Tool stack and data architecture readiness
5. **Customer Outcomes** - Retention, expansion, and advocacy metrics
6. **Governance & Continuous Improvement** - Feedback loops and iteration cadence

Each dimension is scored on a 1-10 scale with detailed rubrics, giving you a clear picture of where you stand and where to invest next.
